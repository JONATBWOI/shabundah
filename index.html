About Us

At Shabunda Minerals and Logistics Ltd, we are committed to revolutionizing mineral transport and supply chain management across Africa.

Our operations span across mining zones, border points, and international hubs. We bring expertise, reliability, and transparency to the mineral trade.

Our Services

⛏ Mineral Site Logistics

We provide tailored logistics directly at the mineral extraction points, ensuring safe and compliant handling from the moment minerals are sourced.

🚛 Ground Transport Fleet

Our dedicated fleet of transport vehicles ensures efficient delivery of mineral consignments, both locally and regionally.

✈️ Air Freight Solutions

When speed is critical, our air freight options guarantee fast and secure shipment of high-value minerals globally.

📦 Supply Chain Optimization

From mine to market, we integrate logistics, documentation, and compliance systems to streamline your entire supply chain.

🌍 Export & Documentation

We manage all documentation and regulatory processes to facilitate smooth international trade and customs clearance.

Whether you're an international buyer, local supplier, or mining cooperative, we ensure seamless logistics and compliance from mine to market.
